Saint Thomas School

... is an educational institution based on American Curriculum and Content Standards certified by AdvancED Southern Association of Colleges and Schools (SACS) of the United States and the Ministry of Education of the Dominican Republic (MINERD). We provide a superior education in Pre-School (1-5 years), Elementary, Middle and High School for students in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic. In our environment students feel safe, appreciated and special.

Saint Thomas School commensurates with the abilities and educational needs of our students body. The student are prepared for the 21th Century with world language and technology instructions. The development of multiple intelligences is stressed through instructions and performance via musical, theater production, science fair, spelling bee and more!

Mission

To create a nurturing learning environment where students, faculty, and community work together to develop the skills and knowledge essential for achieving academic excellence, ethical behavior, and personal responsibility, through a balanced education that will prepare students to contribute to a global society.


Vission

Learning is natural

To be a model school offering a United States standard based education that promotes lifelong learning, academic excellence, and the social skills necessary for the achievement of a fulfilling, productive life.
